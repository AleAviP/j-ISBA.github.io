BAYSM:O

We are pleased to announce the Bayesian Young Statisticians Meeting: Online (BAYSM:O), a conference organized by the junior section (j-ISBA) of the International Society for Bayesian Analysis (ISBA), to be held online on November 17-18, 2020. This conference will provide a platform for the dissemination of excellent work by junior researchers in the field of Bayesian statistics, and allow them to interconnect with the Bayesian community.

Please check back here closer to the conference for the schedule. It will also be announced on our Facebook and Twitter.
